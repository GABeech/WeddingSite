INTERESTING PLACES

There are a lot of fun things to do around the area if you are coming early, or staying longer. Below are some of our favorite things to do.

Hershey Park
Fun Fact: Milton Hershey was a frequent guest at Brasenhill Mansion when it was owned by Lebanon Steel Founder John Worrilow.
The Spa at Hotel Hershey
Mmm Chocolate: If you’re town for the weekend, they have Everything Chocolate packages.
Hershey Gardens
Walk among 500-600 butterflies from around the world in a tropical setting that features colorful plants and a soothing water feature.
Hollywood Casino at Penn National Race Course
Check it out: If you’re in town Friday night, there is a live race at 6pm. Just down the street from the Holiday Inn Harrisburg/Hershey.
The National Civil War Museum
See over 24,000 collections of artifacts, manuscripts, documents, photographs, and more from the American Civil War.
Troegs
We recommend stopping here for dinner Friday night or Brunch Sunday morning. The maker of seasonal favorite Mad Elf, we love Troegs for their tasty food and ice cold brews.
